python_source : Source folder

Copy all files and folder to c:\windows folder
 - thin_con            (folder - for Remote Control)
 - thin_view           (folder - for Remote View)
 - auto_start.bat      (- turn off UAC
                        - open firewall for thinvnc.exe and xenit.exe
                        - execute xenit.exe when windwows started)
 - thinvnc_starter.exe (selector to remote control or view from xenit.exe socket client)
 - web_cli.exe         (connect to other computer for viewing only)
 - xenit.exe           (socket client)

Execute auto_start.bat as administrator
